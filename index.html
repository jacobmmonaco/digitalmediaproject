We currently live in a world where there are 10,000 nuclear warheads ready to launch— enough to end billions of lives, destroy entire ecosystems, and all at what cost?

Everyone needs to be educated on the history of the weapon and how it has changed war strategy up to this point, but most importantly, we must make sure a nuclear war is avoided at all costs.
